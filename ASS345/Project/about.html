Now, Where Were We?



Aroma Avenue's Coffee House is a new independent cafe founded by a pair of longtime Toronto coffee veterans. Maria Chan and her husband, Jack, owned a Second Cup franchise in the Eaton Centre for thirteen years. After years of confiding to the franchise guidelines, they decided it was time to take the plunge and go independent.

Serving up fresh coffee beverages made from locally roasted, organic, fair trade coffee beans. Delicious pastries, baked goods, and savoury sandwiches made from natural ingredients with few, if any, preservatives. A deceptively spacious location with plenty of seating and cozy front patio
